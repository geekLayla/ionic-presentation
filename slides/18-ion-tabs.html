Tabs
Tab is also a very used UI component. it allows us to power the interface with a tab bar and a set of pages. tabs will be shown at the top in android OS and at the bottom in iOS devices. In fact all ionic UI components respect OS specific styles, behaviors and transitions since ionic follows the platform specific guidelines to display elements in the right way. Having specific styles, behaviors and transitions by default in eauch plateform is very nice, but having a way to overwrite then in a single place is even better.
